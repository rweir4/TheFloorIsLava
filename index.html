THE FLOOR IS LAVA

Welcome to The Floor is Lava, inspired by your favorite childhood game.


Touch the floor and you die, but there's also a limited time

If you dont make it past 30 platforms before the timer runs out,

the lava will be your doom.


To play, use these controls:


Left Arrow: Walk Left

Right Arrow: Walk Right

Down Arrow: Drop Down

Spacebar: Jump

Game Over

YOU WON!
